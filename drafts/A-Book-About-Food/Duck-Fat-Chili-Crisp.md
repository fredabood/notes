# Duck Fat Chili Crisp

Chili crisp is super easy to make, and is improved by a high quality, home made animal fat. Duck fat makes the best chili crisp.

# Ingredients

## Fat

- 899g duck fat
- 450g canola

## Aromatics

- 2 dozen cloves - 2g
- 8 star anise - 9g
- 4 cinnamon sticks - 24g
- 5 bay leaves
- 129g garlic

## Spices

- 75g Szechuan peppercorns
- 16g fennel seed
- 13g cumin seed
- 45g sesame seed
- 227g chili powder

Instructions

1. Crisp garlic in oil
2. Remove garlic
3. Fry aromatics in oil
4. Remove aromatics
5. Pour hot fat over spices and stir
6. Salt and sesame oil to taste

# Favorite Places

## Vibes + views

- [London House](https://maps.app.goo.gl/1gcQGee8pJb2TFbq5?g_st=ic) - probably the best rooftop view in the city
- [Cindy’s](https://maps.app.goo.gl/GjntaYNWiycMuozR6?g_st=ic) - same building as the Chicago athletic association
- [Virgin Hotel](https://maps.app.goo.gl/hvJm97skE61sTxPaA?g_st=ic)
- [Gibson’s Italia](https://maps.app.goo.gl/CnVuuef6GKtCAu34A?g_st=ic) - great view, food, and drinks

## Bars with Games

- [Chicago Athletic Association](https://maps.app.goo.gl/mtGiqAz5URRSttYg8?g_st=ic) - swanky spot with fun games
- [Punch Bowl Social](https://maps.app.goo.gl/JEwbnFJUqREeJ7ch8?g_st=ic) - not my favorite vibes but they have every game ever made
- Emporium [West Loop](https://maps.app.goo.gl/9dZyCzreoU29aJds9?g_st=ic) or [Logan Square](https://maps.app.goo.gl/kyVzV1A6Mx7f5FXg9?g_st=ic) Logan Square- barcade
- [G Cue](https://maps.app.goo.gl/M76EdSugtm4LHWqp8?g_st=ic) - billiards in West Loop

## Nightlife

- [Federales](https://maps.app.goo.gl/sNmCPZ5KVMd9KDiu5?g_st=ic) - definitely go definitilythrow an icem shot at the bell
- [Blind Barber](https://maps.app.goo.gl/6hz1dAxEypmaVEgo6?g_st=ic) - gets rowdy AF
- [Boozebox](https://maps.app.goo.gl/vZNhPiXZsKZuJHzD6?g_st=ic) - Japanese speakeasy, my favorite bar in the city, late night spicy tuna, great cocktails, good japanese whiskey list
- [Lazy Bird](https://maps.app.goo.gl/wNE52cKGfbAYkN2w9?g_st=ic) - great cocktails and live music
- [Esco](https://maps.app.goo.gl/PMuTYv4rWggSQ6MH8?g_st=ic) - more clubby
- [Lone Wolf](https://maps.app.goo.gl/yK2ojnrsTDmPvUi26?g_st=ic) - dark bar with cheap drinks on Randolph, I always end up here late night
- [The Bassment](https://maps.app.goo.gl/UTWhbs6bRotg9fnM7?g_st=ic) - maybe my favorite “going out” spot, always great live music
